Start Saving with Kohls Yes2you Rewards Program While Shopping

0

Yes2you Rewards – If you are a regular shopper at Kohl’s then you must read about Kohls Rewards Program. Visit www.kohlsrewards.com to register, activate & see reward status. You must be well aware of Kohl’s products and might be using their products if you are a resident of United States or even you have a short visit to it.

Yes2you Rewards – Intro & Overview

Kohl’s is one of the largest retailers in U.S and they sell a wide range of products ranging from home products to apparel. Kohl’s have been providing their customers with the best quality goods.

Not only, Kohl’s provide you with the best quality, but it blends it perfectly with the reasonable price. So if you are a loyal customer and you always prefer Kohl’s each time, then Kohl’s has a very nice reward program for you. This will help you to save money and get some good items as well.

Kohl’s Rewards Program Registration & Usage Info – Yes2you Rewards

Now to get this amazing reward you will have to register yourself first. Only then you will be able to enjoy these amazing offers.

  1. You need to first enroll yourself if you are yet not a member. Now to begin the enrolling procedure you need to first provide your zip code.
  2. At next you will need to provide your information like birthday, name, address, phone, state, city, email and then hit “Save”. Now one will sign into his/her account or create one at kohl’s.com by using your password and email.
  3. If you don’t have your account, you can get one by entering name and password. Now you are an official member of Kohl’s and you will get $5 on 100 points where each point worth $1.
  4. If you are signed up in store, complete your online enrollment. Your points will expire one year after you have earn them. Visit www.kohls.com for more.
