How to Enroll YourSelf in ASDA Price Guarantee Program

0

www.asda.com – ASDA Price Guarantee program intends to offer a low price if compared to competitors. Visit website to enroll in it & compare. The feeling of regretting the moment, when a cheap product is bought at double price, often makes customers furious.

www.asda.com – Intro & Overview

Most of the people get terribly annoyed when they find that the price of the item they just bought is much cheaper somewhere else. This common problem is faced by almost every customer. At least 4 to 5 times in their lives, but not by ASDA customers. This terrible situation would never happen to people who shop at ASDA stores.

That is because of ASDA Price Guarantee Program. Which gives the customers opportunity to check cost of their comparable grocery and from main it’s competitors. ASDA stores make up the price difference if they are not 10% cheaper. Especially with Tesco, Sainsbury’s, Morrison’s and Waitrose on comparable grocery shopping.

What Is ASDA Price Guarantee Program – www.asda.com

ASDA is only one of the big four supermarkets that are winning the opening skirmishes in supermarket price war. Company reports an increase in like-for-like sales despite pressure on grocery retailers.

ASDA has introduces a Price Guarantee Program for their customers which let them compare the cost of comparable grocery items shopped from ASDA to the advertised price of those comparable items at any of Tesco, Sainsbury’s, Morrison’s or Waitrose. In order for the shopping to be eligible for the ASDA Price Guarantee, the customers need to have bought 8 or more different items, of which at least 1 must be comparable to an equivalent item sold by a competitor. Then, if the total cost of the comparable items is not 10% cheaper than the competitors, then ASDA provides the customer a difference via a voucher.

How ASDA Price Guarantee Program Works

Customers can compare the price of their items bought from ASDA stores online. For that use the Price Guarantee tool, and go through the following process:

  • Visit the ASDA Price Guarantee page and hit the ‘Enter Receipt’ button.
  • Enter the ASDA Receipt barcode number into the required field. The customer must have waited 3 hours after shopping to compare their receipt. The barcode number is between 16 and 22 characters long.
  • The customers need to enter their email address in the required box to receive useful information and special offers in the future and then click on ‘Compare Prices’ button to continue.
  • If the price of the comparable items on the receipt is more than other competitors in results of the comparison, then ASDA will pay the difference plus one penny in forms of voucher or e-voucher.
  • Presenting the voucher and original receipt at the checkout during the next visit to the store or redeeming the voucher online while paying for another shop will enable customers to get the amount off their bill.

After shopping online at ASDA.com, if a customer wants to compare prices, then they must log in to ASDA.com link on the homepage and provide their email address and password and hit on the ‘Compare Prices’ button to continue. 48 hours must have been passed after the delivery of the online order to compare prices online.

Introduction About ASDA Stores

ASDA Stores Limited is a British supermarket retailer chain, headquartered in Leeds, West Yorkshire. The company has spread over U.K. with more than 500 stores. ASDA became a subsidiary of the American retail corporate giant Walmart after a £6.7 billion takeover. Company mainly sells fresh food, clothing, grocery, leisure and entertainment goods, general merchandise and financial services. The majority of the ASDA Superstores contain 35,000 products for the customers to choose from. The company also provides services like pharmacies, café, and some stores may have an ASDA petrol station.
