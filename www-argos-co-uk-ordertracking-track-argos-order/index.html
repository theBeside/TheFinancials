How to Track Argos Order Status Online

0

Track Argos Order – After giving order to Argos & check online status at www.argos.co.uk/ordertracking and see when order is at your door step to unwrap it. People who love online shopping can never take it easy when it comes about waiting for their order sent to the door step and finally being able to enjoy the excitement of un-wrapping the package.

Track Argos Order – Intro & Overview

For such kind of people, tracking their order online and not to wait and worry about anything would be a great blessing. So digital shopping has created a gadget to meet the needs of online shoppers. If someone shops at Argos, they can track their orders easily online.

Argos is a British catalogue retailer of home ware, apparel and general merchandise operating in the United Kingdom and Ireland, and a subsidiary of Sainsbury’s. Argos is the UK’s leading digital retailer, offering more than 33,000 products through www.argos.co.uk. Moreover, Argos is offering its services through growing mobile channels, stores, over the telephone and uniquely, through Argos TV.

Being a part of the Home Retail Group, the UK’s leading home and general merchandise retailer, Argos continues to be the UK’s largest high street retailer online with over 430 million website visits annually. Argos serves around 130 million customers per year through a network of 740 stores.

Tracking Argos Order Status Online

 Argos has eliminated the unwanted waiting and curiosity after making an online order, from the life of online shoppers by providing them with a unique facility of tracking their orders anytime. To view Argos order status online the customer has to click the ‘Order Tracking’ link on Argos Homepage and enter the order number, billing post code and their surname. Then by clicking on the ‘View Order’ button they can check their order delivery status.

Argos has provided the customers with three different ways to make a delivery from Argos. These three ways are online, by phone or visit the store. All the home delivery orders at Argos are given an order number which can start with a number or some letters.

Once a customer places an online order at Argos, they will be issued with an on screen Order Reference number and an email confirmation with their order reference number, product details, price and delivery information.

If someone shops at an Argos store, the order number will be printed on the bottom of their receipt.

If a customer has placed an order over the phone, they will be provided with their order number at the end of the call. If someone is experiencing any kind of problems during the order tracking process, they can easily contact Argos to know where their order is.

Argos Customer Satisfaction Review

Argos is committed to make their stores and services accessible to everyone. If a customer needs any help in shopping with them, they have provided a contact number and Ask in Store facility to make things easier and comfortable for their customers. They also offer an audio CD version of their services, with a selection of their products. A copy of that CD can be purchased by calling to their specific number which does not deal with any other enquiries.

Argos believes in taking a responsible approach to the environment and the local communities. They believe that working to meet these objectives is the best way to live up the expectations of people for them as a retailer, enhance their reputation, and manage their resources effectively in the face of rising costs.
