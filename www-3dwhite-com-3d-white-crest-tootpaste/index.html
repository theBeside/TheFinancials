3D White Crest – Intro & Overview

But a very sad fact is that many people do not smile often or do not laugh openly in public. Because they do not have white teeth or their teeth have surface stains of coffee, tea or smoking. Teeth’s whitening is such a common problem that many people even seek medical treatment to whiten their teeth. This problem was luckily noticed by the American Multinational Company Procter and Gamble (also known as P&G), so they introduced a toothpaste brand named ‘Crest’ in 1955.

Crest is a leading toothpaste brand which focuses on improving oral health. The brand is associated with many dental cares and oral hygiene products including toothpaste, toothbrushes, mouthwashes, dental floss etc. Crest products are made in the United States and sold worldwide. In some countries, Crest is sold as Blend-A-Med.

Crest 3D White ToothPaste

Crest 3D White is one of the successful toothpaste brands of Crest. The Crest 3D White is the first teeth whitening brand recognized by the editors of top beauty magazines. It has Shakira as its brand ambassador. Setting Shakira’s impressive alluring smile as their standard, the brand promises their customers that brilliant 3D white smile, by using Crest 3D white products.

Crest 3D white products consist of 7 kinds of Whitestrips, 5 kinds of Toothpaste. Moreover, 3 kinds of Toothbrushes and 2 kinds of Rinse and Floss. 3D White also introduced the Crest 3D White brilliance collection composed of two advanced products. Which includes Crest 3D White Brilliance Toothpaste and Crest 3D White Brilliance Boost.

Crest 3D White Brilliance Toothpaste contains an “Illuminating Complex”, according to the company. The Toothpaste aims to give shine and luster for a brilliant smile after just one use. It utilizes micro-cleansing whiteners to polish away surface stains and reaches areas between teeth to clean with a gentle foaming action.

The Crest 3D-White Brilliance Boost contains three times the amount of stain lifting ingredient compared to Crest 3D White Toothpastes to target surface stains from coffee, tea, wine, and smoking. The Booster’s whitening ingredient also adheres to the tooth’s surface while releasing stains. The formula is non-fluoride and one can use it in a daily routine.

Other Dental Care Products from Crest 3D-White

Along with Crest 3D-White Brilliance products, Crest 3D-White sells a wide variety of dental care products which provide solutions to specific dental problems. These products include Crest 3D-White Luxe Professional Effects Whitestrips, Crest 3D-White Luxe Diamond-Strong Mouthwash, Crest 3D white Luxe Supreme FlexFit Whitestrips, Crest 3D-White Arctic Fresh Toothpaste and many more products for sensitive teeth.

The whitening results of the Crest Toothpastes normally comes within or after three days. If the results not satisfy you, then simply return the receipt and package UPC within 60 days of purchase.
