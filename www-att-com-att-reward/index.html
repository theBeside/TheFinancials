AT&T Reward Card for Uverse Customers Rebate

1

AT&T Reward: Learn how to redeem, activate & check the AT&T Reward card status at www.att.com and login to proceed with your online account.  When you buy the products or subscribes to AT&T services, you possibly get some rewards. The reward may be is in the form of AT&T Reward Card.

AT&T Reward – Intro & Overview

It can utilize your AT&T Reward Card everywhere (where travel Visa/permit is recognized). It is important to keep watch email inbox and immediately claim the reward before expiration date.

If you want to use the cash from Reward Card, it is required to redeem Reward Card and activate the card online in advance. Then you will be able to check the current reward status online, see the available balance, you can also look at reward eligibility and many other features.

Steps to Redeem AT&T Reward Card Online

You will get a reward notification through your mail with in the first month of your tools installation or your home service connection. It will contain all the redemption directions and regulations for redeeming your Reward Card. The only condition which is very necessary is that you have to keep your service active for a month or any other given time period. The steps to activate AT&T Reward Card are the following;

  • You have to go and click rewardcenter.att.com/myattrewardcard.
  • Now click on “Reward Center Home” in the top navigation bar to get started.
  • Then you have to put the Redemption ID or Confirmation Number (which will be present in the reward notification letter or provide your account number & zip code).
  • Press “go” to carry on.
  • You will be done with the whole process in a small number of inputs.
  • You can observe the reward status, view reward eligibility etc. in the middle of the process.
  • After redemption you will receive your Reward Card in a few weeks or a month.

Important note is that business customers will be not facilitated with this entry. If you want to activate your Business Rewards, you have to type High Speed Internet Telephone Number or U-verse Billing Account Number to get initiated. 3-4 weeks will be required for the Reward Center to show your rewards.  If your reward is qualifying for a purchase of High Speed Internet Direct, then go into your High Speed Internet Direct Account Number.

Activation Procedures of AT&T Reward Card & Check Card Balance

If you have an AT&T Reward Card handy, then you have to activate it for utilizing it. Procedure to activate the card read the following;

  • Go to rewardcenter.att.com/myattrewardcard.
  • Now to activate the card, enter the first four digits of the reward card.
  • Click on the option “Continue”.
  • Enter the complete card number and a four digit PIN which are the last four digits of account number.
  • Follow all the instruction to get it done.
  • .You can confirm the card balance in the same way. You can contact the AT&T customer service number (given at the back of your card) in case of any difficulty.
