USCellular.com/12Days: Win With US Cellular 12 Days Promotion

0

Open USCellular.com/12Days if you want to earn with US Cellular 12 days of giveaways sweepstakes. U.S. Cellular just announced this sweepstakes right after the launch of Match & Win IWG by Tracfone and Selfie Sweepstakes by Cricket Wireless.

It’s an online promotion in which anyone can participate at www.USCellular.com/12Days. If you are a customer of US Cellular then you are eligible for this survey. This promotion will begin in the holiday season if you are a resident at any one of 50 states of America OR District of Columbia. Then you are eligible to take part in this promotion. However, you must be at least 18 years old while taking part in this survey.

Although the registration period starts on November 16. However, they will start giving away from December 1st with the tech-related items at weekdays only. Furthermore, the final drawings of the grand prize will be done on December 18. The prizes of worth more than $20,000 will be awarded during the month from the popular tech brands including Samsung, JBL, and LG.

Visit USCellular.com/12Days to Enter

You can open USCellular.com/12Days and then follow the steps to complete the registration form. To increase the chances of winning you can simply complete the registration form on daily basis. This will increase the chances of winning the grand prize at the final drawings.

Starting from December 1st, every customer of US Cellular is invited at USCellular.com/12Days to play the game. The more you play the game the more there is the chance to win the game. The result will be displayed immediately if you win or not. Each time you play the game, you will automatically enter as a participant to win Grand Prize.

There are total 114 Prizes, which are distributed in a sequence of 12 Prizes Per Weekdays. The prizes will be awarded randomly and the list of the prize with day and date is as followings.

  • Day 10 (Dec. 14): JBL Flip 3 Wireless Bluetooth Speakers

  • Day 12 (Dec. 16): Withings Activité Pop Watches

At the end of the Promotion, the winner of Grand Prize will get a big deal of HOME PRIZE which will worth about $7,500. The Grand Prize will comprise of the following items.
