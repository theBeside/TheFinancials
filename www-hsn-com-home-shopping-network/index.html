FLEXPAY

Really want to buy a thing, but the price is too high? Well, no need to worry now. You can now pay for your purchase in easy installments with Flex Pay. You pay for the first installment, shipping charges and tax using your credit card, debit card or Pay Pal account. The remaining amount is broken down into easy monthly installments that can be paid via the same credit/debit card or Pay Pal account. HSN does not charge any interest fees on Flex Pay.

There are plenty of things that you need on a routine basis, so HSN has provided an option for you to get such things shipped to you automatically without you having to order them again and again.
