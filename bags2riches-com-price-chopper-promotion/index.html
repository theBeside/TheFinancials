Bags2Riches.com: A Contest By Price Chopper To Win $1,000,000

0

Bags2Riches is a Game by Price Chopper for 2018, Visit Bags2Riches.com to take part in the promotion. Get a chance to win $1,000,000 in cash for the first grand prize winner. There are many other prizes also including, 1 in 3 tickets for great food or discounts. Moreover, winners will also get a chance to win a car, free gas for a whole year, and many more things.

The total amount of $6 million is available in terms of prizes and discounts to be won by participants. This amount also includes $1 million of the grand prize. To increase the chances of winning the game, you have to play more. Play Here

Bags2Riches.com – How to Participate in Contest

In order to participate in the contest, you will need to get Bags2Riches game tickets. This contest is for Bags2Riches 2018 period.

  • Participant has to spend at least $25 by using AdvantEdge card at any outlet of Price Chopper or Market Bistro which is taking part in a contest to get 1 game ticket.
  • Purchases of $50 to get 2 game tickets.
  • Purchases of $75 to get 3 game tickets.
  • Max. limit of tickets is 12 in one shopping trip.

Another way to get a ticket is via email, you can simply request a Bags2Riches ticket in an email. Just write up your full name, mailing address, AdvantEdge card number, email address and phone number on a card of 3″ X 5″. The card must also contain some lines about your likings about the Price Chopper store. You can then send the envelope to;

Price Chopper Bags2Riches Number purchase Tickets, P.O. Box 783, Tonawanda NY, 14151-0783.

The limit for mailing is up to 12 Tickets per request, per day, one request per outer mailing envelope.

How to Play Bags2Riches Game

There are about 4 different ways to play the game and be a participant in this promotion by Price Chopper.

– Instant Win: One in three-game tickets to get a food prize or discount on it. If your ticket shows a message “You Win” or Save “$1.00 off” then you will get an instant Win prize or instant Discount. There are about 5,466,715 instant win prizes or discounts available for participants during the entire promotion period.

– Collect to Win: All game tickets will contain 3 collect to win stickers. You have to match stickers to complete any of 11 categories from the collector card. In return, you can win $1,000,000 cash, new Cadillac XT5 SUV, Kitchen Makeover, luxury Cruise, free gas for a year, free groceries for a year, and much more.
If there are any duplicate stickers, then there is no issue. Just clip them up and complete the entry form and mail to Price Chopper Bags2Riches.com address as below.
P.O. Box 784 Tonawanda NY 14151-0784 to participate in a chance to win $5,000.

– Online Sweepstakes: If you get a 9-digit code printed on the ticket, then visit Bags2Riches.com and fill up the registration form. Don’t forget to enter your pin, enroll today to get a chance to win $10,000 cash, Price Chopper gift cards, Free Gas for a year and much more.
